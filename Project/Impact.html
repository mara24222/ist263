His Impact On the Community

Why Should We Care?

This man is everything to our generation. Latin America is huge, and when you're from the Dominican Republic, Puerto Rico, or Cuba, sometimes you feel left out. Every country has a different dialect, but when you're from one of these three countries, your accent can many times be mislabeled as "ghetto" "barbaric" "incorrect" or even "ugly". These three countries experience a lot of bashing and racism from other Latinos. Although sad, it is true. Bad Bunny having the fame that he does, while being Puerto Rican, means everything to the Latino Caribbeans. After being judge for our accents and Spanish DIALECT, we are finally being appreciated. This is our moment, and we have him to thank for it.

Problems In Latin America

Latinos go through enough as is, and homophobia, is a big issue in Latin America, along with Racism!!

What Has He Done To Combat These Issues?

Bad Bunny has done so much to shed light on both these issues, more than many other artists have. He is extremely comfortable with his sexuality and his style. He is a feminist and is not afraid to show it. One of the best examples I can use to support this claim is his video from his song "Yo Perreo sola" one of his most popular songs, in this video he dresses up as women in order to show support for a transgender women who was killed in Puerto Rico. He uses his platform to help Latinos and make them feel accepted and appreciated, no matter their race and or sexuality.
