His Music and My Reviews

He currently has five albums which he created on his own, and one album which was a collab. His albums are, Un Verano Sin Ti, YHLQMDLG, El Ultimo Tour Del Mundo, X100Pre, Las Que No Iban a Salir, and Osais, which is his collab with Jbalvin. He has worked on songs with Artists such as, Drake, and Cardi B.

Oasis is the album he has with Jbalvin, not his best, but still has some bangers.

X100PRE is an amazing album, this album is what really made me love him. Every song on this album is different and shows a raw talent.

This is an album he realesed during quarantine. This album is good but not great.

Un Verano Sin Ti is his most recent album that he released. This album has remained number 1 on the Top Billboards since the day it came out.

YHLQMDLG is one of my favorite albums by Bad Bunny. This is where he really showed his talent and how much range he truly has! I love this album more than I love myself.

Yonguni is a song he realesed as a single and in this song he speaks japanese fluently, which is extremly impressive for a man who only speaks Spanish! This song is beuatiful and very unique. None of his fans expected the japanese and it was a great surpise!
